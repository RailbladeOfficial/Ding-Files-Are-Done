# Sound effect attributions

The "Standard" sound effects (Chime, Ding, Pop, Marimba, Retro Alert, Soft
Bell, Arpeggio, Fanfare, Level Up, Descending Bloop) are synthesized at
runtime with the Web Audio API — no audio files, no licensing to worry
about.

The "Special" sounds are real bundled audio files under `sounds/custom/`,
listed here with their source and license so you have a record of it.
